Our People

Coppercore is lead by a team of highly experienced professionals with a proven track record in the mining industry. With a focus on people, strong values, and a commitment to sustainability, we are dedicated to creating value for all our shareholders.

Antonio Treminio

President & CEO - Venture Capitalist and specialist in private and public equity markets with over 25 years of experience in Wall Street and International financial markets with a special focus on the Natural Resource sector. Proven ability in team building and empowerment of individuals in the advancement of investors and/or shareholder value.

Eric Schjelderup

VP of Strategic Planning - Eric Schjelderup is a highly accomplished professional. As a UK/Canadian national, Eric brings a global perspective to his role. President of NASC Europe. Representing Chinese companies wishing to go public. Advisor to Zaige Group in Hebei Province, a recycling company.

From Dec.1988 to the present, Eric has also served as President and CEO  of Port Mercantile Capital Limited (HK), Invested and raised $10 million for a Philippine-based mobile banking company and installed banking technology in Metro Bank and Union Bank. He listed China Energy, a prominent Chinese coal company in Inner Mongolia in the USA. He raised $25 million for an offshore gas project with CNOOC providing gas to Wenzhou, China. He has extensive relationships in China and Hong Kong. From May.1985 - Nov.1988 Eric was President and CEO of West Pacific Business Capital Ltd. In Vancouver, Canada,  raised $135 million for eCharge Corp. Seattle, USA. Eric was also Vice President of Arakis Energy, which raised $800 million for successfully constructing a pipeline (1600KM) project in Sudan during its civil war and listed on NASDAQ. He also listed United Collision Centers (UCA), Jewitt Cameron, and Amtronics Inc. He and UCA also sponsored a race car at the 1988 Indianapolis 500. Eric’s business expertise includes corporate strategy, situation analysis, going public processes, investor/public relations, private placements, and capital raising.

Eric’s education: Lahr Institute (Dept. of National Defense) Lahr, West Germany,  Mount Royal College, Calgary, Alberta, Canada.

Daniel Ringuet

Senior Advisor & Co-Founder - Mr. Ringuet is a seasoned investment banker and venture capitalist with a wealth of experience in listing private companies in the Canadian Capital Markets. With over 35 years of experience in the financial markets, he has provided invaluable assistance and advice to numerous public and private companies, from the venture stage to the development stage, in their quest for capital and human resources. Mr. Ringuet has cultivated an extensive network in North America and Europe and has also served as a management-level advisor or board member for several companies.

Juan Antonio Sefair

Director of Financing - Juan brings over 30 years of experience in the financial sector, with a focus on value generation and financial analysis. Daniel O'Farrill Amescua

VP of Commercial Diplomacy Mexico - Daniel, a financial industry veteran with 12 years of experience in Mexico, specializes in structured and alternative financing.
